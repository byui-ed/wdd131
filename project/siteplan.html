Site Plan for Farmers' Market Guide

Site Name

FarmFresh Local Market

Chosen because it emphasizes freshness and locality, appealing to community members seeking fresh, local produce and vendors.

Site Purpose

This website serves as an informative guide to our local farmers' market. It provides visitors with key details such as operating hours, location, vendor profiles, seasonal produce, upcoming events, and a way to contact the market organizers. The goal is to promote local agriculture, support community engagement, and make it easier for visitors to discover and participate in market activities.

Target Audience Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: A local resident wants to find out the market's operating hours and location to plan their weekly shopping trip.
  • Scenario 2: A visitor interested in local produce wants to learn which vendors will be present during the upcoming seasonal market and what products they offer.

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #2e7d32 (Green) - Used for headings, buttons, and accents.
  • Accent Color: #0288d1 (Blue) - Used for links, highlights, and interactive elements.
  • Background Color: #f9f9f9 - Light grey background for the body.
  • Section Background: #ffffff - White background for content sections.

Typography

  • Headings: 'Georgia', serif
  • Body Text: 'Arial', sans-serif
  • Highlights or Special Sections: 'Courier New', monospace

Wireframe Description

Mobile View: Single-column layout with header, main content sections stacked vertically, and a hamburger menu for navigation.

Desktop View: Wide layout with header, navigation menu, main content divided into sections, possibly with sidebar for quick links or filters.
